1:1 Classes

A one to one class is lovely way to build your confidence before joining a public class, or perhaps to take your regular practice to the next level. Each session will be tailored to meet your needs, giving you space to ask questions and go at your own pace.

Depending what suits you we can do these classes at your home, or via Zoom.

If you are interested please get in touch and we can arrange a time for an informal chat. This will be an opportunity to express your needs and hopes and ask any questions you may have. Please contact sineadoyoga@gmail.com.

Sinéad is caring and attentive and she has really helped develop my technique.
