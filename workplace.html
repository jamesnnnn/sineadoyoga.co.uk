Yoga in the Workplace

Working for long periods at a desk and looking at screens all day can be both draining on your body and energy levels. However, it can be tricky to fit a yoga class in around a busy work schedule and home commitments.

Yoga in the workplace can be a lovely well-deserved break for your team. Using my previous experience of working in the mental health sector my yoga classes encourage people to prioritise their well-being and take some time away from their desk and ‘to do lists’ to recharge.

I offer uplifting and nourishing classes, enabling your team to release built up tension from desk hunching and general work pressure.

If you are interested in offering yoga classes at your workplace please get in touch at sineadoyoga@gmail.com and we can find a time to suit you and your team. The classes can be in person or online.
